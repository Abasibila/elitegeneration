Supplier of cultural Quality Baskets from Bolga

The Elite Generation Baskets company Ltd. is a retail and Wholesales supplier of cultural baskets woven by the indigenous people of Bolgatanga, a region in Ghana.

About Elite Generation Baskets company

The Elite Generation Baskets Company aimed to preserve the culture of Bolga baskets, especially the Guresi community in the Upper East Rigion of Ghana

The company has been operating for some decates now. There are about more than 5000 weavers enrolled into this company where gender inclusivity is a major pillar

Each basket was woven by an artisan. No special tools were invulved except the hand (purely handcrafted), we appreciate every uniqueness in each and every piece. Thanks to Bolga baskets artisans.

Moreover, we look forward to present to you more beautiful and attractive baskets. By achieving this, more technical training including colour combination is given to our artisans. The training will also take care of quality of baskets to enhance durability.

About Our Founder

The Elite Generation Baskets Company was founded by Abasibila Edward Ayamdoo. He started the company's root within the boundaries of Ghana and later extended to other parts of the world. Edward got interested into basket business after his high school education, though, he has been a weaver from his ealy ages. He thought that his company could be a chance of supporting the poor talented local weaver in Ghana, as well as extending thier beautiful products to other parts of the world.
